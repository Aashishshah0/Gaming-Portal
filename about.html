About Us

What's a Gaming Portal?
Thank you for reading Gaming Portal, a news and opinion site about games and things serious gamers care about. We’re here to inform you and, sometimes, entertain you. We also often write about pop culture topics like anime, TV, and movies: if it’s good, interesting, or inspires us somehow, we want to tell you about it. We aim to be an inclusive site for gamers of any ethnicity, gender or sexual orientation. We expect our writers and commenters to treat those they write about as they would if they met them in person.
