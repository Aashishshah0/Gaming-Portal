Essential Podcasts for Gamers
Pick up a controller and jam those earbuds into your ears.
Published in Jan16, 2023| Gaming Portal |
Gaming is a well-established medium with a robust online community, but podcasts, which offer a powerful space to niche voices, have made engaging with games and other gamers easier than ever. While some people seek out places for the latest news and reviews, others want content that’s more thoughtful—discussions about the history of gamings, navigating the feelings we have about playing them, and how they can be more inclusive for marginalized people. The following podcasts invite you to view the gaming world from a variety of perspectives, from industry experts who have been talking about games for decades, to fresher voices who are trying to rework the industry and fandom from the inside. All of these creators provide a welcoming place to talk games—the ones that elicit feelings of nostalgia, the ones we can’t wait to buy, and the ones that frustrate the hell out of us.
Earwolf & Heather Anne Campbell, Nick Wiger
Get Played Heather Anne Campbell, Nick Wiger and Matt Apodaca love video games. Join them as they joke about the games they're playing, have played, and will play someday. Good games and bad games: It's time to Get Played!
IGN
Game Scoop! IGN.com shoots a week’s worth of gaming news straight into your ear!
NeebsGaming
Neebscast This is the OFFICIAL podcast of Neebs Gaming! We talk about LIFE, LOVE, and the pursuit of gaming with Friends and an amazing community!
Kinda Funny
Kinda Funny Xcast
Xbox Podcast The podcast for Xbox fans. We’re giving Xbox-focused insight on Xbox news, we're reveling in nostalgia, and we're making predictions about the future. It’s granular about the Xbox ecosystem. Hosted by Mike "Snowbike Mike" Howard, Star Wars Rogue One writer Gary Whitta, and Parris Lilly. Watch the free video version at YouTube.com/KindaFunnyGames
Maximum Fun
Triple Click Video games: They’re serious business! Except when they’re not serious at all. Each week on Triple Click, video game experts Kirk Hamilton, Maddy Myers, and Jason Schreier journey into the fascinating world of games. They’ll explore hot topics in video game news and culture, answer burning listener questions, debate the pros and cons of the biggest new games, and replay old classics together.
